About us

Fogie concept is considered one of the leading procurement and logistics partners in Africa With 3 offices across the country, including the head office in Lagos, Nigeria. The company also has operations in North America. Fogie Concept was established in 2004, with a single aim in mind, to provide the most seamless procurement and logistics process there is. Although our work is multipurpose, we offer competitive, cost-effective and advanced solutions to our clients. We offer a selection of services that include sourcing, supply chain solutions, construction, procurement, and supply of heavy-duty equipment, machinery, instruments, tools, spares, materials, chemicals, and other consumables. Our footprints can be tracked across various industries, from oil and gas & engineering to government parastatals; our clients span various industries. It is at the center of our ethos to source and procures the right quality tools, materials, and equipment for the people in need of it and we are on a mission to establish ourselves as the best logistics and procurement service provider in our industry.
